# Listing Committee

### Roles and Responsibility

Critical proposals is still decided by governance voting in [Snapshot](https://vote.cream.finance), but light proposals will be decided by Listing Committee.

Light proposal:

* Asset Listing
* Collateral Factors
* Collateral Caps
* Reserve Factors
